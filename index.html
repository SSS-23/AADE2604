About AADE

Headquartered in Singapore and registered with the United Nations ECOSOC, the Asia Academy of Digital Economics (AADE) is the leading platform for digital economic research and global exchange in Asia. Our work focuses on three strategic missions:

01

Digital Inclusive Development

Fostering synergy between industry, academia, and research to democratize innovation and accelerate the translation of inclusive digital solutions.

02

Digital
Connectivity

Bridging borders and sectors by connecting industry leaders, scholars, and global experts through high-impact dialogues to align mindsets and synchronize digital strategies.

03

Digital Governance
& RegTech

Advancing human-centric digital governance and RegTech to cultivate a transparent, inclusive, and trustworthy digital landscape for all.

Since 2023, AADE has accelerated digital economy transformation worldwide.
